How to build Bard

Locket of the Iron Solari


+20 ability haste
+200 health
+30 armor
+30 magic resistance

Devotion: Grant allied champions (including self) within Range 850 units a Hybrid resistances shield for 180 - 330 (based on target's level), decaying over 2.5 seconds (90 second cooldown).

Consecrate: Grant allied champions within 850 units 3 bonus armor and 3 bonus magic resistance.

Mythic Passive: Empowers each of your other Legendary items with 2 bonus resistances for this item's passive effect, Consecrate.

Locket of the Iron Solari since it fits the best with the Bard's utility playstyle and runes. The shield you get is extremely good, especially against burst, dive and heavy engage, try to time the shield with the aforementioned descriptors. Locket will scale well with Conditioning and the bonus health from Overgrowth. The aura passive isnt strong, but scales later with teamfights, making your team tankier passively.

Force of Nature


+350 health
+70 magic resistance
+5% movement speed

Taking magic damage from champions grants a stack of Steadfast for 7 seconds, stacking up to 6 times with the duration refreshing on subsequent magic damage from them and whenever dealing damage to them. Becoming Stun icon.png immobilized by an enemy champion grants 2 stacks and also refreshes the duration. Damaging basic attacks and spells per cast instance can only grant 1 stack of Steadfast every 1 second.
While at 6 stacks of Steadfast, gain 10% bonus movement speed and reduce all incoming magic damage by 25%.

Force of Nature's components are ideal to build after Locket because they grant both health and movement speed this is ideal for roaming around the map and making plays. The passive builds on this, more MR, and more move speed inside the teamfights. However this is only if they have more AP dmg, than AD dmg.

Frozen Heart


+20 ability haste
+400 mana
+90 armor

Every first incoming instance of post-mitigation basic attack damage per cast instance is reduced by 5 (+3.5 per 1000 maximum health), maximum 40% reduction each.
Cripples the attack speed of enemies within 700 units by 20%.

Frozen Heart has been the best armour item in the game for years. This has been a main stay Bard item for as long too. If the enemy team is AD heavy and/or Auto attack reliant this item's value only increases. It does so much for stats, except give health, but with the runes we build and invest into, they make up for it and Bard should have about an extra 200hp by the time the game ends, thereby effectively bypassing Frozen Hearts biggest "weakness".

Shard of True Ice


+40 ability power
+75 health
+115% base mana regeneration
+3 Gold per 10 seconds

Warding: Consumes a charge to place a Stealth Ward at the target location, which grants sight of the surrounding area. Charges refill upon visiting the shop (4 charges; 600 range). Bard can have 3 stealth wards active on the map at any one time.

This is the support warding item you will want to build on Bard 9/10 times. unless you are in a vunerable matchup where both laners are outranged severely. The stats aren't great and pale in comparison to the desirable stats of the alternatives, but this item has the potential to stack the fastest and therefore come online faster in time for you to roam around the map and make plays and create picks and advantages for your team as the second jungler.

Wardstone


+15 ability haste
+150 health

This item can store up to 3 purchased Control Wards Control Wards.
Increase your Stealth Ward, Totem Ward, and Control Ward caps by 1.
Increases bonus attack damage, bonus health, ability power and ability haste by 12%.

These last two items have been nothing more than Bard ward slots, usually speaking Bard cannot rely upon teamates to ward the map, therefore worst case scenario make sure you have as many wards at your disposal. The best times to build this is definitely when the enemy team has at least one stealth champion, every team fight you will have to put a control ward down, so that the stealthed enemy doesnt find a good flank angle. The other cases to build the additional ward item is when deep vision is needed, against champions that have unique gank paths or flank angles like Pantheon, Zac, Rek'sai, Kayn.
