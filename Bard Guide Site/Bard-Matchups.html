Champions to pick Bard with

Bard lane phase is very much impressed upon positioning and gracefully losing, not falling too far behind, you did well. Its rare that you will win lane phase, but in the situations that you do, you should be playing so much better than the opposition that the rest of the game shouldnt be much pressure, especially around the mid game powerspike.
Bard struggles with even and stacked fights bot lane. Bard doesnt have much damage and as such, his identity relies around staying safe and fundamentally laning smart with an in and out trade pattern and stunting the opponent with shrines and stuns. Outside of lane phase, Bard loves to skirmish with an advantage, make picks and cause chaos through clever vison. Paired with champions who can skirmish and snowball or both is ideal, they can play off of Bard's lack of damage and scale faster than the curve if Bard plays the map well post 14minutes.

ADCs -



Caitlyn: Caitlyn offers range and relative safety with her traps and net. Bard being a short to medium ranged champion needs to be offset with range. Her CC traps can also be set up for free with Tempered Fate and Cosmic Binding double stuns. Vice Versa, Caitlyn's traps and slows set up your trades and CC.



Ashe: Ashe offers nearly as much range as Caitlyn, but the upside is that Ashe's abilties and autos either slow or stun... Just like Bard's autos and abilities. Playing this lane is very safe and deceptively good at winning trades off the back. They also have huge global set up together, with dual semi global ults and team peel.



Jhin: Similar to Caitlyn with traps and utility set up, but is more vunerable without an escape in kit. Jhin ult however is usually countered by other champions standing in front of the initial target, Bard ult puts the protecting teamate into stasis, negating the attempt.

JUNGLE -



Graves: Bard excels in the mid game with skirmish heavy junglers that have the potential to make up for Bard's lack of damage and make use of his utility. Graves puts out a huge amount of damage in the jungle due to his Q interactions with wall, just like Bard, they set each other up. Graves also likes to get resources and get kills and be aggressive, when mid game comes around or even just roam timings, you are both boiled down to a roaming bot lane. The drawback is that Graves is also short ranged, so be careful of ranged junglers, disengage and those who are able to kit around.



Amumu: Setting up ganks has never been easier. Two AOE ultimates that work off of each other, two Q's that both stun for additional setup and both tanky with passives that increase damage. A match made in heaven, that carries over to team fights later too



KAYN: Few champions define Skirmishing from the jungle role as well as Kayn. Snowballing into either Red or Blue doesnt matter the ability to get Kayn online faster and hit the champions stride faster is invaluable. As Red Kayn, Kayn will benefit from the additional shields, heals and shared tank stats, as Blue Kayn the set up CC into a quick assassination or disengage.



LILIA: Similar to Amumu, Bard and Lilia are both able to set each other up through their ultimates. Bard's additional slows also help the Lilia kite and hit her sweetspots in early skirmishes. The only disadvantage is the fact that the CC is very telegraphed, but if you are hit by a slow, a harder form of CC is en rout with Bard Q, or Lilia ultimate, putting multiple people to sleep, into a Tempered Fate after the teamfight breaks down into an additional Skirmish. On the flip side, landing a Bard ultimate, into Lilia hitting multiple champions with Drowsy is an instant teamfight win. This however is really dependant on proficiency

MIDDLE -



FIZZ: Zhonya is a CORE item on this champion. Bard has a free Zhonya every 70s/80s mid game. This grants the fizz about 4s of untargetable freedom to get his CDs back up. His ultimate chains to yours nicely and vice versa, it also slows to help set up stuns besides this. Fizz is a huge snowball champion that loves a skirmish too.



TWISTED FATE: Point and click stun into a double stun from Bard. Some of the easiest set ups Mid and Bot there is. Both like to roam in the chaos of Solo Q, the ability to have more than just roaming champion can be helpful, just make sure that there is damage elsewhere because otherwise, there may not be enough raw damage in the comp.



RYZE: With two ultimates that are semi global, moving around the map and making rotation/macro plays are the backbone Bard's greatest identity is moving around the map and creating picks, with Ryze at the helm with a realm warp, the co ordinated Pair should never need to tramfight. With constant roams mid Bard can help Ryze through his worst point too; his early laning

TOP -



ORNN: I have a huge personal bias toward Ornn and his playerbase. The late game Ornnaments are amzing for stat value and Bard is nothing more than a stat stick, he needs nothing more than synergistic stats. The set up that Ornn has with Bard is great too another far reaching ultimate that can engage from over a screen away. Ornn also have a passive that reduces the tenacity of enemies he hits with CC. Bard already technically has one of the longest CC chains in the game (Q,R,Q) if you land it, this will keep a priority target locked down for longer than 7s. In that time they will be dead, but it works wonders against champions like Tryndamere, who cannot die for a portion of time or Kled.



GWEN: Having someone in the side who is good at sidelane and teamfight is the type of versatility that thrives with Bard. This gives him more angles to play off of and create picks. Gwen also has slows within her kit so she is also able to help peel, kite and set up Bard for anything he need to achieve in the pick.



PANTHEON: Similar to Ryze and Twisted Fate, but from the top lane, Pantheon excels at creating picks and skirmishes with advantage his ultimate, globablly teleports him toward someone out of position. Use this in conjunction with Bard's mobility combined with both champions early game damage, this is a recipe for success.

Champions that counter Bard


Bard is blindable, therefore there doenst exist any specific chamion that excels against Bard once you have the fundamentals in order, however too much hard engage early is something that Bard doesnt like. Also stealth, that being said its no surprise that champions like: Leona, Blitz and Naut are to be played against very smartly in the bot lane. Similar to Pyke and Thresh, his own playmaking potential is easier to play around with those two and they offer far more kill threat in the bot lane early. Asides from this, champions with extreme mobility to escape from your stun angles and ultimate: Akali comes to mind very quickly, so does khazix, but he is rarely seen. Without point and click lockdown CC like a Maokai, navigating these matchups is highly reliant on deep warding and smart cross map plays and bot lane snowballing. Control Wards are mandatory here.

Champions that Bard Counters


This should be prefaced by stating that Bard doesnt hard counter much outside of punishment of out of postion carries, especially those without inherent escapes: Miss fortune, Aphelios, Kog Maw, Ashe etc. Getting an early flash out of any of these and more, and layer them for the next 5 minutes with on CD ults every time u see them with an ally in the wing to help u kill them. Within lane however Bard does well against other supports and carries that won't punish his carry as he roams or offer much kill threat in lane. This isnt to be confused with being able to playing Bard into enchanters, because they scale just as well if not better dependant on the patch, items and such. The way to counter that is to make sure if you dont want to teamfight, make sure you dont teamfight, make picks and work around vision with your skirmishers before a fight to force an advantage.
