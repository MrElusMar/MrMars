Passive-Traveller's Call and Meeps

Ancient Chimes-Small chimes appear throughout Summoner's Rift attracted to Bard's presence these Chimes will spawn in relative proximity to Bard. Passing over them grant several temporary buffs: 24% movement speed out of combat (Lasts 7s, stacks up to 5 times each chime grants an additional 14% move speed stacking up to 80%ms), 20 +1 per minute post 5 minutes level experience and 12% maximum mana. Bard empowers his auto attacks with each Chime he collects, granting buffs each 5 Chimes Bard collects.

MEEPS!-Bard attracts Meeps to his side, starting the game with one by his side. As he collects more ancient chimes throughout the map Bard gains more access to Meeps. Bard's auto attacks deal extra damage with his Meeps. Every 5 chimes deals 35 + 14 per 5 Meeps collected + 30%AP bonus magic damage on hit. Plus these are some additional effects:

There are some important breakpoints with this passive:

At 5 Chimes collected, Bard's auto attack slows enemies by 25% for 1s
At 15 Chimes: Bard's Meep empowered auto attacks will deal splash damage behind the initial target slowing all affected enemies
At 25 Chimes: Slow = 35%
At 35 Chimes: This splash damage radius is increased behind the initial target
At 45 Chimes: Slow = 45%
At 60 Chimes: Slow = 55%
At 75 Chimes: Slow = 65%
At 85 Chimes: Slow = 75%

Using this passive doesnt take much in the way of active use. The best utilisation however is to make sure that our chime count mirrors out vision score count; 2 per minute. Next is to make sure that when we auto later on when we have more meeps to space out our autos because they have a CC component in that they slow, and slows don't stack, so wait for the slow to wear off, then re apply the CC. Make sure to roam and hit the breakpoints as you move around the map and try to time them for important fight, skirmishes and picks.

Q-Cosmic Binding

Mana: 60 Mana
Cooldown: 11/10/9/8/7
Cast Time: .25s
Target Range: 850
Skillshot Width: 60
Speed: 1500
Damage: 80/125/170/215/260 (+65%AP)
CC Duration: 1/1.2/1.4/1.6/1.8

Bard fires cosmic energy as a straight line skillshot, damaging the first target it hits and carries on behind for the same distance. If the skillshot collides with another enemy, wall or structure, both are stunned, otherwise a slow of 60% is applied.

New Bard player tips: Do not use this ability in lane unless you are going for the stun. This ability wastes mana needlessly as a harrassment tool. Bard has good auto attack damage with his passive and during the later stages of lane phase (post 6mins) should have 15 Meeps meaning Bard also does splash damage on hit with autos. This ability should only be used in lane as a peeling tool.
Cosmic Binding double stun hit boxes are deceptive at best, learning the angles will take time, but once mastered you can stun from some very weird angles resulting in stuns in places that will surprise ally and enemy alike.

W-Caretaker's Shrine

Mana: 70
Cooldown: 14
Cast Time: .25s
Target Range: 800
Min Heal: 25/50/75/100/125(+30%AP)
Max Heal: 50/87.5/125/162.5/200(+60%AP)

Bard creates a shrine at the target location that when stepped on by an ally heals them, when stepped on by an enemy destroys it. Bard can have upto 3 shrines active on the map at any one time. These shrines charge over 10s granting more HP as they charge. If a 4th shrine is placed, the 4th shrine will replace the first one placed (FIFO) but if targetted directly onto an ally it will not, because it will be coded as a direct heal. Bard and his allies that are healed by the shrines gain +30% MS over 1.5s post heal.

New Bard Player Tips: This ability is great putting a second point into at level 4, the value doubles and the mana stays the same. This in turn helps mitigate Bard's subpar lane phase, also will help keep your ADC healthy whilst you roam. Leaving some mind mid lane as you roam back and forth isnt a bad idea either. Placement wise, make sure to place them not too far away, a good general placement is in line with the caster minions and/or along escape paths.

E-Magical Journey

Mana: 30
Cooldown: 22/20.5/19/17.5/16
Cast Time: 0.25
Target Range: 900
Speed: 900/1197
Bard opens a one wall tunnel through any eligble terrain, he and his allies can travel through this tunnel faster than enemies, hence the two values. The tunnel will last for 10s total.

New Bard Player Tips: There are a lot of pieces of terrain on the map that will require practice to move through. The skillshot indicator will need to be dragged through the entire terrain. Positoning next to walls with Bard is great for escaping and setting up plays, this might be one of Bard's hardest aspects of his kit, but pivotal to playing Bard well. Do not waste this, the CD is decently long and portaling affects both teams, so DO NOT give flank angles for the enemy unless you follow up with a Cosmic Binding or Tempered Fate or for bait.

R-Tempered Fate

Mana: 100
Cooldown: 110/95/80
Cast Time: 0.5s
Target Range: 3400
Effect Radius: 350

Bard sends cosmic energy arching to a target location, granting sight during travel. Upon impact it puts all units caught stasis for 2.5s all units hit are revealed for the statis duration.

New Bard Player Tips: This ability has some of the highest mastery ceilings the game has to offer and deserves a guide itself. practice makes perfect. As a general rule ult the enemy backline in teamfights. One word of caution, be careful about conflicting ultimate usage. Some ally champions will be decimated by your mistimed ultimate usage. So think before ulting and make sure there are no conflicting goals at the time. Every ult should try to land and create pressure, this is Bard's signature ability. Make or break, get a flash, get a kill or save an ally. No inbetween.
